TYPES OF TABLE

1.Permanent Table:

	a.Time Travel retention period  0-90 days
	b.Fail safe

2.Transient Table
	a.Time Travel retention period  0-1 day
	b. No fail safe

3.Temporary Table
	a.Time Travel retention period  0-1 day
	b.No fail safe
	c.Exists only in the current session.Once worksheet or session is closed these table is also gone.
